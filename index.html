About Me

My journey into leadership coaching and speaking wasn't a straight line. For over ten years, I was immersed in the world of tech and engineering, and what I discovered was a passion for a different kind of leadership—one that's built on collaboration, trust, and a deep sense of purpose. Today, I have the privilege of partnering with leadership teams across a wide range of organizations, from innovative corporate teams to mission-driven schools and churches. Together, we move beyond outdated systems to build a new model of leadership that creates real impact and truly serves those it leads.
